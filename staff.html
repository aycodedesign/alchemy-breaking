ATN

Etienne Carreira started his on-going 24 year professional dance career during his teenage years while attending the prestigious Las Vegas Academy of the Performing Arts where he attended & majored in dance by day while entertaining people on stage at night with his first show Imagine at the Luxor hotel.

His invaluable work ethic, personality, attention to detail & self-awareness made him an asset to every project in which he was a part of. Etienne relocated in 2001, alongside his brother & continued to train under Flo-Master, Kujo, Tara Foy, Tavres Wilson, Roger Lee & Sky Hoffman. While in Los Angeles, he booked various national commercials & industrials with ATT, Coke, Skechers & Dodge. He also has worked with A-List artists Jessica Alba, Pink & Jennifer Lopez on film & on stage & has worked with industry powerhouses Dave Lachapelle, Tabitha & Napoleon D’umo & Kenny Ortega.

Etienne has an extensive breaking pedigree as one of the founding members of the world famous “Battle Born” Crew. He has paved the way for his crew to travel & compete both nationally & internationally, winning both national & international world titles in competitions such as Battle of the Year, World of Dance - San Diego, B-Boy Summit, Outbreak & Style Elements Anniversary.

Dubbed “the Footwork King,” Etienne has battled, judged and taught workshops in 18 different countries over the span of 5 different continents.

Currently Etienne is a featured dancer in Cirque du Soleil’s Michael Jackson ONE at the Mandalay Bay Hotel & Casino, while previously working on the Beatles LOVE at the Mirage Hotel & Casino. He has also previously worked the Jabbawockeez Mus.i.c show at the Luxor, Revealed with Toni Braxton at the Flamingo as well as the legendary show Splash which ran at the Rivera.

Etienne more recently has geared his focus towards dance education by opening up his own dance school the Alchemy Breaking Academy (formerly known as “The Breaking Academy) in 2019 alongside his brother & two partners Kareem Gwinn & Alex Delgado.

From there Etienne he continues to share his passion for dance & knowledge by teaching for the esteemed Joffrey Ballet School intensives & is on faculty at Millennium Dance Complex Las Vegas where he & his brother hold a weekly Breaking class centered on professional dancers looking to train & widen their scope of movement as well as a second class focused on technique with a student base which is all ages from kids to adults.

GEOM

Hailing from Hawaii, Guillaume Carreira is a second generation dancer born from a family of professional performers & artists.

In 1997, he moved to Las Vegas where he entered high school as a dance major & refined his classical training in ballet, jazz & modern at the prestigious Las Vegas Academy of the Performing Arts.

Guillaume began his professional career at 16 years old as a performer for one of the longest standing shows in Las Vegas, Folies Bergère at the Tropicana Hotel as a dancer/acrobat.

To expand on his career, Guillaume relocated to Los Angeles in 2001 where he booked numerous national commercials with American Eagle, AT&T, Coca-Cola, McDonalds, Toyota, Target & more as both a dancer & as an actor. The move to Los Angeles continued to be a fruitful pursuit as he has gone on to do multiple world tours including the critically acclaimed musical, STOMP. He has toured in all 50 states and in over 20 different countries.

Most recently, Guillaume has been working in Las Vegas as a soloist in the Beatles “Love” by Cirque du Soleil for the past seven years at the Mirage Hotel & Casino.

He has also choreographed for Cirque du Soleil including their annual show One Night for One Drop with John Legend, as well as Michael Jackson ONE at the Mandalay Bay.

Guillaume is also a co-founder of the world famous breaking crew “Battle Born” where he has won a number of national and international competitions with his crew including the Sup3rcrew Anniversary & World of Dance San Diego as well as winning the titles of R16 World Bboy Qualifying Champions in Seoul, South Korea & Battle of the Year USA Qualifier Champions which brought them to compete against the top teams in the world in Montpellier, France.

In 2019, both Etienne & Guillaume opened up their own dance school the Alchemy Breaking Academy (formerly known as “The Breaking Academy) The program initially focused on teaching breaking techniques for students from kids to adults & has now grown into multiple extensions of dance concepts in their pursuit to bring breaking to a much wider student base, pushing students who normally focus on choreography in classical dance styles to really think & dance “outside the box” to diversify their movement vocabulary.

Guillaume continues to enjoy sharing his experience & knowledge both on & off the floor with dancers of all experience levels & all ages. His unique background in transitioning from a classically trained dancer makes him so well versed & helps make what seems like a completely intimidating dance style much more approachable.

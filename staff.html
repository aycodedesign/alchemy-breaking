Etienne Carreira

Co-founder, Instructor

Etienne "ATN" Carreira is currently a featured dancer in Cirque du Soleil's show "MJ One." He has previously worked for Jennifer Lopez, Pink & the Jabbawockeez & has taught breaking at programs for Millennium Dance Complex & the Joffrey Ballet School Summer Intensives. He has won numerous competitions both nationally & internationally as a solo dancer & with his breaking crew, Battle Born.

Guillaume Carreira

Co-founder, Instructor

Guillaume "GEOM" Carreira is currently a soloist in Cirque du Soleil's show "Beatles Love." He has previously worked & toured with the show "Stomp" & has danced for the Jabbawockeez show "Mus.i.c." Kareem Gwinn

Co-founder, Instructor

Kareem Gwinn is currently a professional dancer & a competitive b-boy with his crew, Rock Force. Originally from the Bay Area, his accolades include "B-Boy Of The Year" from World of Dance & was an official performance selection at the Sundance Film Festival in Park City, Utah. Kareem holds both numerous national & international championship titles from Warsaw, Poland to Los Angeles, California. He also works as a physical therapy assistant in the Las Vegas area when he is not travelling.

Philip Teixeira Albuquerque

Instructor

Philip Teixeira Albuquerque aka Spee-d (Instructor) is an all-round B-Boy, but especially known for his amazing power and flips. Spee-d is of Brazilian heritage and has been doing capoeira for about 16 years.

Spee-d started dancing about 18 years ago. He got into breaking because it was something new and fun to do. He wanted to learn the amazing powermoves because he always liked anything acrobatic. Throughout his B-Boy career, he always stuck more to power, but throughout years, he came to truly appreciate all aspects of the dance and started working really hard on his foundation—not just footwork and dancing, but also foundational power.

Philip got the name “Spee-d” during his capoeira days. He dances solo and with his crew and has performed in numerous commercials, for Pepsi, Target, Visa, Comcast, Alltel, K-Swiss, Mc Donalds and more. He also has been in such movies and tv shows like Shake It Up, Dancing with the Stars, So You Think You Can Dance, America’s Got Talent, Epic Movie, Meet the Spartans, Dance Flick, Honey 2, Step Up 3, Jack and Jill and many more. Spee-d was born in the US to Brazilian parents. He speaks both English and Portuguese. His family supported him in whatever he did and they are very proud of his career. He finds great backing in his crew “Unique Styles” too and calls his wife, B-Girl Dama, his number one supporter. He says because of her he has become the B-Boy he is today.

Spee-d has been competing and judging in battles all over the world—in France, Portugal, Finland, Korea, Italy, Spain, and many other countries and he plans to keep on battling. Currently he lives in Las Vegas performing for Cirque Du Soleil in their very famous show KA.

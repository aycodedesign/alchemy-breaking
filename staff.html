Etienne Carreira

Co-founder, Instructor

Etienne "ATN" Carreira is currently a featured dancer in Cirque du Soleil's show "MJ One." He has previously worked for Jennifer Lopez, Pink & the Jabbawockeez & has taught breaking at programs for Millennium Dance Complex & the Joffrey Ballet School Summer Intensives.

He has won numerous competitions both nationally & internationally as a solo dancer & with his breaking crew, Battle Born.

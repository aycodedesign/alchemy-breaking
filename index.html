WELCOME TO ALCHEMY BREAKING ACADEMY

Established in 2019 originally as “the Breaking Academy,” which began as a dance program geared towards students who wanted to learn the basic fundamentals of "Breakin’.'" Class sessions revolved around learning foundational concepts of this dance style such as top rocks, footwork, freezes, power moves & cyphering. In addition to learning not just the dance, students will also learn discipline, self-confidence, strength, trust, practice & team work all while discovering their own individual styles throughout the program fostered by our instructors who have over two decades plus worth of experience as professional dancers.

In Fall of 2020, the company re-branded & are now known as the Alchemy Breaking Academy, to further solidify our identities within the dance community. Breaking had previously been seen as a “street dance” which didn’t belong in traditional types of studios & classical settings. Our mission is to educate & show those who once saw this particular style of dance as inferior to other dance styles as something of greater worth in numerous different kinds of applications.

We have since expanded our basic program into multiple programs which include a “Breaking Choreography” class as a way to bridge the gap between dancers who mainly train choreography, an after-school program, workshops, hosting dance events & competitions, private lessons, virtual learning, open floor sessions, visual direction for print & digital campaigns.

We invite everyone of all ages & all experience levels to come & move with us at our many locations located throughout the Las Vegas Valley. Move with us & come be a part of the “New Gold Standard.”
